# Interval Timer

An iOS app for anaerobic and high-aerobic interval training.

## The Problem

Most interval timers on the App Store are designed for steady-state cardio. They handle "work 30s, rest 30s, repeat" well — but they break down when you need:

- Asymmetric work/rest ratios for anaerobic intervals (e.g., 20s all-out, 90s recovery)
- Multi-phase workouts with warm-up, working sets, and cool-down
- The ability to save, edit, and revisit structured workouts
- Audio and haptic feedback timed for high-intensity training
- Quality execution

## The Solution

A purpose-built timer that treats interval training as a real domain, not an afterthought. Designed for atheletes.
